Overview

Goals:

This app is designed to help you prepare to Hash Code competition.

Rules

Score:

Your score depends on the problems you solved, submissions and Coins.
Your score will determines your rank.

Coins:

Everyone starts with standard amount of Coins.
You can earn coins by solving problems.
Ps: the amount of Bonus Coins will be decreased whenever you submit a wrong output.

Cost:

Each hint will cost you 50 Coins
Each solution will cost you 150 Coins

Levels:

Each level except the tutorial will be opened right after its resources are public.
